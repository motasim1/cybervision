Frequently Asked Questions

What are strikes?

Strikes are a kind of punishments that are used in our server. If you do something bad, you will get a strike. Strikes are a kind of warnings.

How can I get a staff role?

Every staff member in our server has applied for the staff role and went through the process you will need to go through. You need to apply for the role, then a higher staff member, management probably, will review it. If they find that you will be a good staff member, will they add you to the waiting queue. When the server needs more staff members, will they choose someone of the queue.

How can I change my nickname?

If you don't have the right permissions to change it for yourself, you can do "-nick [your new nickname]". This will allow our staff team to either accept it or deny it. All the nicknames must follow the rules.

I have a strike, how can I remove it?

You can appeal all your strikes, kicks and bans that you got on our server. A staff member will review the appeal, and accept it or deny it.
